Privacy Policy – theBeetles

This app is used internally by our organization for team-building activities, onboarding, and company events. We value privacy and aim to keep all user data secure and limited to its intended use.

Information We Collect

  • Team names (used to group participants)
  • Photos submitted by users during challenges

How We Use This Data

Submitted photos are temporarily stored to evaluate challenge participation and calculate event scores. Team names are used only within the context of the event.

Data Retention

All submitted photos are deleted from our Firebase storage shortly after each event ends. No personal data is retained or shared.

Your Data

theBeetles uses Apple’s sandboxed image picker, which only accesses images explicitly selected by the user. The app does not request access to the full photo library.
